Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to the Staff Leave Portal. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when using our WhatsApp notification services.

1. Data Collection

We collect and store employee names, team names, and phone numbers solely for the purpose of internal attendance management and notifications. This data is provided by your organization's administrators.

2. Use of WhatsApp

We use the Meta WhatsApp Cloud API to send automated notifications regarding leave requests, work-from-home (WFH) approvals, and daily attendance summaries to authorized managers and employees.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold or shared with any third parties outside of the Meta (WhatsApp) notification infrastructure required to deliver our services.

4. Security

Our database (Excel-based) is stored securely on our internal servers and access is restricted to authorized personnel.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact your internal IT or HR department.
